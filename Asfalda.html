Target notional domains

Annotated frames pertain to the following notional domains:
  • Causality (specific Causation/Evidence disambiguation guide)
  • Cognitive positions
  • Commercial transactions
  • Verbal communication (partially annotated)
Encountered occurrences not pertaining to these domains were annotated with a special Other_sense frame.

Origin of the frames

Frames in the French FrameNet are either copied or adapted from Berkeley FrameNet release 1.5. More precisely:
  • Frames not starting with 'FR' are taken directly from FrameNet 1.5, but only core roles and non-core but subcategorized roles were retained.

  • Frames starting with 'FR' are either new frames, or modified frames from FrameNet 1.5
Only Core roles (=essential semantic roles) and non-Core but subcategorized roles are annotated.

Frame annotations on syntactic treebanks

The frame and roles have been annotated on top of the morpho-syntactic annotations of:
  • the Sequoia Treebank (Candito M. and Seddah D., Le corpus Sequoia : annotation syntaxique et exploitation pour l’adaptation d’analyseur par pont lexical, TALN 2012, Grenoble, France).
  • the French Treebank (Abeillé, A., Clément L., and Toussenel F. 2003. "Building a treebank for French", in A. Abeillé (ed) Treebanks, Kluwer, Dordrecht)
To obtain all the layers of annotations (morphological, syntactic, semantic), contact marie.candito at gmail.com.
